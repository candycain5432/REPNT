Return Policy

Returns are accepted within 14 days of delivery. Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in original condition. To begin a return, email us with your order number and reason for return.

Customers are responsible for return shipping costs unless the item arrived damaged or incorrect.

Shipping Info

Orders are processed within 2–3 business days. Standard shipping typically takes 5–7 business days within the U.S.

You’ll receive a confirmation email with tracking once your order ships.

We currently ship to the U.S. only.
