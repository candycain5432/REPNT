About Us

Welcome to REPNT. Clothing.

This isn’t just fashion—it’s faith in motion.

REPNT is a Christian streetwear brand built to represent truth, purpose, and bold belief. Every piece we drop is designed to make a statement—about who you are and who you live for. We believe in turning heads, not just with style, but with the message of Christ.

Whether you're in the city, at church, or just living life, wear your faith loud. This is more than clothing. It’s a calling.
